Why Clean Water?

  • Health: Clean water prevents disease and saves lives.
  • Education: Kids, especially girls, can go to school instead of fetching water.
  • Opportunity: Clean water empowers entire communities to thrive.

Our Mission

Every day, millions of people wake up knowing they will spend hours walking to collect water that isn’t safe to drink. This daily struggle robs children of their education, parents of their livelihoods, and families of their health. At charity: water, we believe that clean water is a basic human right—and we’re on a mission to bring it to everyone, everywhere.

"771 million people lack access to clean water. Together, we can change that."

With your help, we’ve already funded thousands of water projects in communities around the world, transforming lives and creating brighter futures. But there’s still so much more to do. Join us in making clean water a reality for everyone.
