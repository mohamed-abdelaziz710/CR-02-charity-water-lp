Why Clean Water
Changes Everything

Health

Clean water reduces waterborne diseases by 80% and protects children from early mortality.

Education

When clean water is available, children can go to school instead of spending hours fetching water.

Women Empowerment

Women and girls spend hours collecting water. Providing water gives them time for education and work.

Economic Growth

Every dollar invested in water and sanitation projects returns $4 to the local economy.

A Life Transformed by Clean Water

Fatima used to spend 5 hours daily collecting water for her family, which deprived her of education and childhood. The water she collected was contaminated and repeatedly made her younger brother sick.

After a well was built in her village, Fatima's life changed completely. She now attends school regularly and dreams of becoming a doctor to help her community.

"Water is life... Now I have time to study and dream of a better future. My brother no longer gets sick from contaminated water. We can grow crops and earn income, and I return from school to see my family safe."

- Fatima, 12 years old
