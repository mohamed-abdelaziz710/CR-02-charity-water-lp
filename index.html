Why Clean Water Changes Everything

  • Health: Clean water reduces diseases and protects children.
  • Education: Kids can go to school instead of fetching water.
  • Women Empowerment: Women have more time for education and work.
  • Economic Growth: Water projects help the local economy.

A Life Transformed by Clean Water

Fatima used to spend 5 hours daily collecting water for her family. The water was contaminated and made her brother sick. After a well was built, Fatima now attends school and dreams of becoming a doctor.
